Can I submit notes that are written across scattered pages?

Yes, absolutely. Just ensure that when you use your scanning app (like CamScanner) to compile them into a single PDF, you scan the pages in the correct chronological order so that Chapter 1 reads before Chapter 2.

What engineering fields/departments do you format for?

Our templates and technical library fully cover Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering courses at the Jinja Campus. Each department report is custom-written with its unique technical language.

What happens if my supervisor requests corrections later?

KweeraX offers 100% free structural revisions for up to 3 days after delivery. If your computer supervisor asks you to adjust margins, change a heading level, or re-align a table, just message us and we will tweak it instantly.

Why do you provide both a Word document and a PDF file?

We give you the PDF so you can immediately see the perfect layout and print it without font issues. We provide the editable Microsoft Word (.docx) file so you can easily type in minor personal edits or make quick changes down the road.

Is my payment transaction and training data safe with KweeraX?

Completely safe. Your mobile money deposit goes directly through the secured telecom network string. Furthermore, we maintain strict privacy—your industrial training data and company details are never shared or resold to any other student.

Will my report look exactly like another student's report?

Absolutely not. Every single student has a completely unique industrial training story. We use your specific logbook notes, company department, and field tasks to generate a custom technical narrative. No templates are recycled, ensuring zero plagiarism flags from the department.

How do we handle engineering drawings, circuit diagrams, or site plans?

We format professional, standard figure placeholders with exact academic captions (e.g., "Figure 3.1: Circuit Layout"). We leave the precise space blank so that after you print your report, you can cleanly draw your diagrams by hand or attach your printed schematics directly into the document.
